Message from Director, IQAC

Greetings from the Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of the ZNRF University of Management Sciences (ZUMS). Our goal for the IQAC is to directly influence the caliber of our graduates by supporting and advancing high-quality teaching, learning, and research. Higher caliber graduates will create more responsible global citizens and a workforce that is better equipped. Quality assurance has become a widely recognized way of education in higher education around the globe.

It is imperative that our graduates meet international standards with competence. In response to the growing demand for high-quality education, the ZNRF University of Management Sciences (ZUMS) founded the IQAC on February 07, 2023; ZUMS-IQAC has embraced outcome-based curriculum as the main practice of the institution and has been trying to acquaint stakeholders with outcome-based education (OBE) from its founding; It will combine more general concerns of tolerance, diversity, sustainability, and environmental responsibility among our graduates besides enhancing their skill set.I'm happy to announce that IQAC has been a permanent educational outlet of the ZNRF University of Management Sciences (ZUMS) since February 2023. It operates as a stand-alone facility and is committed to the overall quality assurance and enhancement of the ZUMS. The University has reconstituted the Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) in March 2024 with a view to further strengthen quality assurance work during extended part of ZUMS’s start-up phase of academic and extra-curricular activities. I look forward to your valuable remarks, support and collaboration on this extension of start-up phase. With your help and gracious collaboration, IQAC at ZUMS has the potential to become a world class higher education in the in the quality full education arena.
