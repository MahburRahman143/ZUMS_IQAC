About Us:

These days, higher education quality assurance is a global challenge. It is therefore imperative that quality full higher education is closely matched with community needs as well as graduate needs to explore employability and learning opportunities. Aware of the growing demand for quality in higher education, The Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) is established in every public and private university of Bangladesh to create a system for systematic program reviews and guarantee acceptable standards for teaching, learning, research, knowledge creation, and academic and administrative support services. At The ZNRF University of Management Sciences (ZUMS), the Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) was established on February 07, 2023. To achieve its aspirational vision of being an exceptional center for higher education in Bangladesh and beyond, ZUMS's IQAC is fostering an exceptional culture in both academic and administrative support roles.

Vision:

Assist in assuring and enhancing quality of higher education at ZUMS to gain national and international recognition as a world class university by achieving academic excellence with innovation, research, exceptional teaching through practices of Quality Assurance (QA) standards and benchmarked quality criteria.

Mission of IQAC:

  1. To provide a sustainable mechanism for Quality Assurance and enhancement and Quality Assurance (QA) standards according to national and international accreditation guidelines.
  2. Ensuring best practices of the university’s all wings for both national and international Accreditation Requirements.
  3. creating and maintaining an atmosphere that supports excellent teaching and learning as well as Quality of Education 
    4.  
  4. Implement efficient programs and procedures for staff assessment in order to improve both academic and non-academic staff Competency.
  5. Encourage faculties, administrators, and students to be inclusive and accountable.

Objectives of the IQAC:

The objective(s) of the IQAC will be to-

  1. Assist the university in fulfilling its mission by offering quality full education.
  2. Create a framework for deliberate, dependable, and catalyst action aimed at enhancing university administration and academic achievement.
  3. Review existing standards and processes while serving as the university's core quality assurance cell Adopt and incorporate best practices, assist in the development of new standards, policies, systems, processes, and procedures of the University
    4.  
  4. Improve teaching quality and achieve excellence via internationalization, as well as to institutionalize the finest QA activity methods.
  5. Evaluate and enhance community services, research, teaching-learning, and effective governance; Prepare the University to meet the external quality assurance assessment and accreditation requirements; Create and keep an information database on the quality control of all programs and parties involved.
  6. Monitor and evaluate Self-Assessment (SA) practices and processes through appraisal, survey and other mechanisms
  7. Organize training, workshop, seminar on quality assurance for faculty members, non-teaching officers and staff.
